About Us

Redefining Veterinary
Care in East Africa

Vet On Call was founded on the belief that high-quality veterinary care should not be limited by geography or inconvenience. We bring fully-equipped, expert veterinary services directly to homes, farms, and communities — because your animals deserve the best, wherever they are.

  • Licensed veterinarians with over a decade of combined field experience
  • Mobile units stocked with diagnostic equipment and medications
  • Serving urban households, peri-urban communities, and rural farms
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees or travel surcharges

Responsiveness

We move fast. Emergency or routine, timeliness is non-negotiable.

Genuine Care

Every animal is treated as if it were our own. Compassion drives everything.

Professionalism

Qualified vets, rigorous protocols, and continuous learning.

Accessibility

Breaking barriers to quality vet care for all communities, urban or rural.

Our Mission

To make world-class veterinary care accessible to every animal owner — delivered with speed, expertise, and genuine compassion, right where they are.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted mobile veterinary network — a model of care that bridges the gap between professional veterinary medicine and every community that needs it.
